RECIPES

GRILLED CHICKEN

Ingredients: 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, 2 tbsp olive oil, 2 cloves garlic (minced), 1 tsp paprika, 1 tsp dried thyme, 1 tsp dried oregano, 1/2 tsp salt, 1/4 tsp black pepper, juice of 1 lemon, fresh herbs for garnish. Instructions: Preheat grill. Mix olive oil, minced garlic, paprika, thyme, oregano, salt, pepper, and lemon juice. Coat chicken with the marinade and refrigerate for 30 mins to 4 hours. Grill chicken for 6-8 mins per side until internal temperature reaches 165°F. Rest chicken for a few minutes before serving. Garnish with fresh herbs if desired. Enjoy juicy grilled chicken with your favorite sides or use it in salads, sandwiches, or wraps.

Okra Soup (Healthy Local Recipe from South Sudan)

Ingredients: 1 lb okra, 1 lb beef/chicken, 1 onion, 2 cloves garlic, 2 tomatoes, 2 tbsp peanut butter, 2 cups broth, 1 tbsp palm/vegetable oil, 1 tsp cayenne pepper, salt, pepper, fresh cilantro/parsley for garnish. Instructions: Sauté onions and garlic. Add beef/chicken, tomatoes, and okra. Stir in peanut butter and broth. Season with cayenne pepper, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 20-25 minutes until okra is tender. Adjust seasoning. Garnish with fresh herbs. Serve hot. Enjoy this traditional South Sudanese okra soup for a nutritious and flavorful meal.

Matoke with Groundnut Sauce (Healthy Recipe from Uganda)

Ingredients: 4-6 green bananas (matoke), 1 cup roasted peanuts (ground into a paste), 1 onion, 2 tomatoes, 2 cloves garlic, 1 tsp ginger, 1 tsp curry powder, 1 tsp paprika, 1 cup vegetable broth, 2 tbsp vegetable oil, salt, pepper, fresh cilantro/parsley for garnish. Instructions: Boil and cook banana chunks until tender. Sauté onions, garlic, and ginger. Add tomatoes, spices, and groundnut paste. Simmer with vegetable broth. Add cooked bananas and coat with sauce. Simmer to meld flavors. Serve hot, garnished with fresh herbs. Enjoy this healthy and flavorful Ugandan dish.

BEST DISHES

Greek Salad (Greek)

Greek Salad (Greek): Ingredients: 2 tomatoes (diced), 1 cucumber (diced), 1 red onion (thinly sliced), 1 bell pepper (diced), 1 cup Kalamata olives, 1 cup feta cheese (crumbled), 2 tbsp olive oil, 2 tbsp lemon juice, 1 tsp dried oregano, salt, pepper. Toss vegetables, olives, and feta in a bowl. In a separate bowl, whisk olive oil, lemon juice, oregano, salt, and pepper. Drizzle dressing over salad. Serve chilled. Caprese Salad (Italian): Ingredients: 2 large tomatoes (sliced), 8 oz mozzarella cheese (sliced), fresh basil leaves, 2 tbsp balsamic glaze, 2 tbsp olive oil, salt, pepper. Arrange tomato and mozzarella slices on a platter. Tuck basil leaves between the slices. Drizzle with olive oil and balsamic glaze. Season with salt and pepper. Serve fresh. Grilled Salmon with Steamed Vegetables (Scandinavian): Ingredients: 4 salmon fillets, 1 lemon (sliced), 2 tbsp dill (chopped), 1 tbsp olive oil, salt, pepper. Brush salmon with olive oil, season with salt, pepper, and dill. Grill for 6-8 mins per side. Steam a mix of seasonal vegetables. Serve salmon with vegetables and lemon slices. Roasted Chicken with Roasted Root Vegetables (French): Ingredients: 1 whole chicken, 4 carrots (peeled and cut into chunks), 4 parsnips (peeled and cut into chunks), 2 potatoes (cut into chunks), 1 onion (cut into wedges), 4 cloves garlic, 2 tbsp olive oil, 1 tsp dried thyme, salt, pepper. Rub chicken with olive oil, thyme, salt, and pepper. Place chicken and vegetables on a baking sheet. Roast at 400°F for 1 hour. Serve hot. These European-inspired recipes offer a taste of different countries while focusing on fresh ingredients, lean proteins, and vibrant flavors for a healthy and delicious meal.
